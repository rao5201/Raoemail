一、项目概述 | Project Overview

Raoemail 是一款 AI 驱动的安全、高效、轻量化电子邮件服务系统，支持临时邮箱、智能收发、AI 辅助、多端同步，面向个人与企业提供一站式稳定邮件解决方案。
Raoemail is an AI-powered, secure, efficient, lightweight email service system that supports temporary emails, intelligent sending/receiving, AI assistance, and multi-device synchronization, providing one-stop stable email solutions for individuals and enterprises.
